### Support Leveling Targets for Senior Escalation Engineers (SEEs)

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th scope="col">Expectation area</th>

<!-- tickets -->
    <tr>
      <th colspan="2">Ticket Work<br>(Routinely carries appropriate workload size and complexity)</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Ticket Volume</th>
      <td>Becomes involved in a fair share of escalated tickets (relative to the SEE cohort), with a focus towards assisting colleagues overlapping similar work hours, where possible.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Complexity</th>
      <td>Skews their time spent toward higher complexity (e.g. level 3) tickets</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Minimum Public Comments</th>
      <td>Ensures tickets see an appropriate reply cadence, such that a customer is never wondering what the situtation might be. This is true whether the SEE is the assignee of a ticket themselves, or if they are enabling someone else who is replying</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Priorities</th>
      <td>Works on significant and unique issues where analysis of data is complex and may require taking into account intangible factors.</td>
    </tr>
<!-- training -->
    <tr>
      <th colspan="2">Training<br>(Actively provides training to others)</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Onboard/Buddy Training</th>
      <td>May provide buddy guidance to new team members where required, as well as training team members to perform the buddy role</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Squad Training</th>
      <td>Delivers commentary on ticket retrospectives, aimed at helping squads benefit from insights, lessons learned, future opportunities etc.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Technology Training</th>
      <td>Assists in the planning and execution of squad training where subject matter expertise and experience is a fit.</td>
    </tr>
<!-- documentation -->   
    <tr>
      <th colspan="2">Documentation<br>(Works towards building knowledge based artifacts)</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Processes</th>
      <td>Participates in the design and evolution of SEE team processes and tooling, including leading projects in this area.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Playbooks</th>
      <td>Works in conjunction with SMEs within squads or the wider Support team to develop playbooks for categories of support problems.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Projects</th>
      <td>Building on opportunities identified through the course of escalations, contributes to GitHub.com documentation (e.g. PRs in docs repositories), as well as support documentation and knowledge bases, both internally- and exterally-facing where applicable.</td>
    </tr>

    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Project involvement</th>

    </tr>

    </tr>

    </tr>

    </tr>

        </ul>
      </td>
      </tr>

        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
