Career Aspiration Prompts (Optional deep dive to reflect on career direction and fulfillment. These prompts are intended to encourage thoughtful discussion and planning.)

1. Accomplishments: What do you hope to accomplish throughout your career?  
2. Impact: How would you like to create impact? This can be in your role, team, or organization.  
3. Skills & Roles: What skills and/or roles do you need to focus on to accomplish these aspirations?  
4. Potential Next Role: If there was an opportunity, what future role would highlight your talents and help you work on the things that bring out the best in you?  
5. Role Change: Are you considering a role change sometime in the near future? Now, 6 months, 1+ year?  
6. Most Fulfilling Aspects: What aspect(s) of your current or previous roles is most fulfilling to you?

Example:

1. Strengths to Build On  
Reflections: “Led a cross-team migration project that reduced issue tracking delays by 25%.”  
IDP: “Build on strength in leading cross-team projects by taking on more complex migrations.”

2. Growth Areas / Skills to Develop  
Reflections: “Found it challenging to balance urgent vs. high-volume tickets.”  
IDP: “Improve prioritization and time management under competing demands.”

3. Short-Term Development Goals (6–12 months)  
Reflections: “Plan to lead upcoming GitHub Enterprise migration as part of core priorities.”  
- IDP Goal: Strengthen project management skills.  
- Steps to Build: Take internal PM workshop; shadow senior PM on migration.  
- Support Needed: Mentorship from PMO; access to project planning tools.

4. Longer-Term Goals (1–3 years)  
Reflections: “Delivered consistently on security-focused priorities.”  
IDP: “Develop deep expertise in security compliance to become a go-to SME.”
