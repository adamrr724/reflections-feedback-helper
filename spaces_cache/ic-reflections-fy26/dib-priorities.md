# DI&B Priorities

Diverse & Inclusive is one of our Core Values. At GitHub, we seek to build and sustain a workplace and product that reflects the diversity of the global developer community we serve. We know that diverse teams generate more innovative results. We are committed to an inclusive, accessible, remote culture where all Hubbers and developers belong, are valued, and can do their best work.

The Reflection Experience utilizes a specific template and process in Workday. It's a series of questions, Reflecting Back and Looking Forward.

When reflecting back, consider how you activated your DI&B Priority. Where were you successful, what might have you done differently and what will you continue to do to create a diverse and inclusive culture?

When looking forward, decide the focus areas and actions you will take to meet your commitment to a diverse and inclusive GitHub. Below are examples of focus areas you may want to consider:

- **Reflect:** Become familiar with our GitHub DI&B Strategy and apply what you learn to your day-to-day job, particularly on our Inclusive Capabilities: curiosity, courage and awareness.
- **Learn:** Choose from the learning options on the DI&B Pathways by personalizing your learning journey. Apply that knowledge to your everyday at work.
- **Include:** Regularly seek out perspectives from people with a wide variety of backgrounds as a part of practicing growth mindset.
- **Engage:** Actively participate in Community of Belonging (CoB) events, be respectfully curious and make connections.
- **Invest:** Dedicate time in mentoring relationships, as a mentor and as a mentee.
- **Build:** Develop, implement inclusive products, or Ship to Learn inclusive products, processes or practices.
- **Give:** Mentor others - volunteer in a cause close to your heart and make a difference in the lives of others.
