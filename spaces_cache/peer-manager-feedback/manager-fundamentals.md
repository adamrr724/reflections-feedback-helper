Managers have a huge impact on company and team performance, engagement, and retention. Managers by nature are in a position of influence and have a disproportionate impact on everything around them by the coordination dependent on their role. We expect our managers to deliver the results expected of you and your team, grow and develop GitHub employees, and cultivate and uphold our culture where everyone can do their best work.

## Manager Practice: Model, Coach, Care.

### Model: Foster an Inclusive Culture
GitHub managers demonstrate strength of character as role models of culture, values, and leadership. They set the tone with their words and actions and lead with integrity to protect individuals and the company. They are accountable both for business results and to others.

### Coach: Deliver Results through Teams
GitHub managers help the team and others develop a sense of shared purpose through a deep understanding of the broader company context, objectives, and opportunities. They create an environment of empowerment that enables others to succeed through collaborating, learning, and adapting. They foster innovation and an openness to quickly experiment and learn from failure.

### Care: Grow and Develop Hubbers

GitHub managers value and care about others. They are thoughtful and deliberate talent- and team-builders. They create open and trusting environments where people can thrive and possess a sincere interest in the well being, aspirations, growth, and success of others.
