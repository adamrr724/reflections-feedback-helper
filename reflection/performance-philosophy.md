## Performance = ImpactPerformance = Impact

Successful performance is not about activity, it is about the impact of those activities. We recognize impact at all grades and award the highest rewards to individuals with the highest impact that fiscal year. While we perform and deliver impact amidst challenges and risks, we also transform by learning, self-reflecting and considering perspectives from others. This is how we continually improve.

## Bringing Performance & Development to Life

Note: Implementation of our Performance & Development approach may vary in some countries as required by local law.

Continuous learning and insatiable curiosity are embraced. Uncertainty is leaned into, risks are taken, and the journey to learn is embodied—while outcomes are owned. Feedback, new ideas, and fresh perspectives are actively sought, especially from those whose experiences differ from our own. It is a year-round process and is brought to life through the following touchpoints:


### Feedback
Get feedback from a variety of sources like 1:1's & input from the year-round feedback tool, to help activate a growth mindset.

### Reflections
Engage in Reflection Conversations with your manager focused on your impact (results and behaviors) for the past period, your opportunities for greater impact, what you learned, and what you will focus on going forward.

### 1:1s
Have frequent discussions throughout the year with your manager to discuss progress against key deliverables, learning and how you are applying it. Share your interests and areas of curiosity, career aspirations, and goals going forward. These are also opportunities to discuss roadblocks and strategize on how they can be solved.

### Annual Rewards
Annual Rewards are one way that we recognize impact. At the end of each fiscal year, your manager assesses your impact and recommends rewards, which can include merit, bonus, and stock (based on eligibility).

### Promotions
Promotions are one way we recognize and invest in Hubber development and careers. It is an increase in grade that can happen when the scope and job responsibilities of one's current role expands or when Hubbers take on a different role at a higher grade.

### Career Fulfillment
Hubbers are empowered to own their Career Development. Working with their managers, Hubbers are able to explore development opportunities based on their interests, talents, performance and where the organization sees their highest potential. Hubbers can focus on career development and share their aspirations using the Career Fulfillment resources.
